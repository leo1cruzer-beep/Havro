Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

During the waitlist phase, we collect your email address only. When you use Havro, we collect the content of your conversations — your stories, promises, and reflections — solely to provide the service back to you.

How we use your data

Your email is used only to notify you when Havro is ready. Your conversations are used only to provide Havro's memory and reflection features. We do not use your data to train AI models sold to third parties, show you advertising, or build profiles for external purposes.

Who can see your data

No one except you. We do not sell, share, or expose your data to third parties. Havro staff may access anonymised, aggregated usage statistics to improve the product — never individual conversation content.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored encrypted at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard security practices. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your data with the seriousness it deserves given what you entrust to Havro.

Deletion

You can request full deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@havro.app. When you delete your data, it is removed completely — we do not retain backups of deleted content.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: hello@havro.app. We will respond within 5 business days.
